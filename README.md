# Prata-C-Journey

Hello to everyone reading this! My name is Alexander, and I am 31 years old. Last year, I enrolled at TUSUR University, majoring in **09.03.04 "Software Engineering"**.
During my first year, I began learning the basics of programming, with **C** serving as our foundational language. However, I found the university's presentation style a bit lacking for my taste, so I decided to seek a more comprehensive source of knowledge. I chose Stephen Prata's book, **"C Primer Plus"**, as my primary guide.
In this repository, I will be publishing my solutions to the exercises found at the end of each chapter. By creating this repo, I want to document my journey and progress in understanding the language and the "fundamentals" of programming in general. 

I hope to conquer it! :3
